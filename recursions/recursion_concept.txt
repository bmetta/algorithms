Recursion: is the process of defininf a problem or (solution to a problem)
in terms of (a simpler version of) itself

Ex: Find your home
  1) If you are at home, stop moving
  2) Take one step toward home
  3) Find your way to home

Parts of a recursive algorithm
  1) Base Case (i.e: when to stop or bottom part of recursive program)
  2) Work toward Base Case
  3) Recursive Call
